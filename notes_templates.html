Project Planning Template

Use this template to outline your group projects and ensure you address all critical steps.

Project Title: ________________________________________________

Team Members: _____________________________________________

Objective / Research Question:

State the goal of your project and the main question(s) you aim to answer.

Data Sources:

List the datasets or APIs you will use, including any SQL databases.

Methodology & Tools:

Outline the analytical methods (e.g., EDA, modeling) and tools (Python, SQL, Excel) you will use.

Timeline & Milestones:

Break down the project into tasks with due dates (proposal, data cleaning, analysis, report).

Deliverables:

Specify what you'll submit (notebook, report, presentation) and who is responsible.
